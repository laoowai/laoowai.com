Teaching courses

教学课程

物资经营管理

Introduction to Materials Management

Students will learn to optimize resources, minimize costs, and enhance customer satisfaction.

本课程介绍了企业内部物料管理的基本概念和技巧。课程内容包括库存控制、采购、存储和分销。学生将学会优化资源、降低成本、提高客户满意度。

Book: Introduction to Materials Management by Steve Chapman, Tony Arnold.

质量管理系统

Quality Management System

Students will learn to implement and maintain quality systems to ensure high product and service standards.

本课程介绍质量管理体系的原则和实践，强调持续改进、客户导向和流程管理。学生将学会实施和维护质量体系，确保产品和服务达到高标准。

Book: Managing Quality: An Essential Guide and Resource Gateway 6th Edition, by Barrie G. Dale, David Bamford.

零售管理

Retail management

Students will develop skills in managing retail businesses, enhancing customer experiences, and driving sales growth.

零售管理课程深入探讨零售业的动态世界，包括商店运营、商品陈列和营销策略。学生将培养管理零售业务、提升客户体验和推动销售增长的技能。

Book: Retailing Management, 9th Edition 9th Edition by Michael Levy, Barton A Weitz.

市场营销原理

Marketing Principles

Students will learn to create effective marketing plans to reach target audiences and achieve business objectives.

本课程介绍市场营销的基本概念和策略，重点关注市场调查、产品开发、定价、促销和分销。学生将学会制定有效的营销计划，以达到目标受众和实现业务目标。

Book: Marketing: The Core 9th Edition by Roger Kerin, Steven Hartley.

商务沟通

Business Communication

Students will develop the ability to convey clear, concise, and persuasive messages in professional settings.

本课程研究商业环境中的有效沟通策略，强调书面和口头沟通、倾听技巧和跨文化沟通。学生将培养在专业场合传达清晰、简洁、有说服力信息的能力。

Book: Business Communication: Developing Leaders for a Networked World 3rd Edition by Peter Cardon.

国际供应链管理

Global Logistics and Supply Chain Management

Students will learn to design and manage efficient, resilient, and sustainable supply chains in a global context.

本课程探讨全球物流和供应链管理的复杂性，重点关注国际贸易、运输、仓储和风险管理。学生将学习在全球范围内设计和管理高效、有弹性和可持续的供应链。

Book: Global Logistics and Supply Chain Management 4th Edition by John Mangan, Chandra Lalwani.

运输管理实务

Transportation Management Practice

In this course, students will learn about practical aspects of transportation management, including various modes of transportation, carrier selection, route planning, and regulatory compliance. The course emphasizes real-world applications and strategies to optimize transportation efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

在本课程中，学生将学习运输管理的实际方面，包括各种运输方式、承运人选择、路线规划和法规遵从。课程强调现实世界的应用和优化运输效率和成本效益的策略。

Book: Transportation: A Global Supply Chain Perspective 9th Edition by Robert A. Novack, Brian Gibson.

Russian Language

俄语基本语法知识

Russian Language for Beginners course is a comprehensive program, based on the Русский язык как иностранный (базовый уровень). The course is designed to provide students with essential knowledge and skills related to Russian language basics, including vowels, consonants, intonation patterns, adjectives, pronouns, verbs, and simple sentences.

Throughout the course, students will participate in interactive discussions and activities to reinforce their learning. By the end of the program, students will have gained essential knowledge and skills related to Russian language basics, allowing them to communicate effectively in basic situations.

俄语初学者课程是一个全面的课程，以Русский язык как иностранный (базовый уровень)为基础。该课程旨在为学生提供俄语基础的基本知识和技能，包括元音，辅音，语调模式，形容词，代词，动词和简单句子。

在课程期间，学生们将参与互动讨论和活动，以加强他们的学习。到课程结束时，学生们将获得有关俄语基础的基本知识和技能，使他们能够在基本情况下有效地沟通。

Business & Logistics courses

商业与物流课程

Business and Logistics courses is a comprehensive program, based on the syllabus of Cambridge International AS & A Level Business 9609. The course is designed to provide students with a deep understanding of the intersection of business and environmental concerns, as well as essential knowledge and skills related to management and marketing.

Throughout the course, students will participate in interactive discussions and activities to reinforce their learning. By the end of the program, students will have gained a comprehensive understanding of business and environment, as well as important skills related to logistics, management, marketing, and business management.

商业与物流课程是一个综合性的课程，根据剑桥国际AS和A级商业9609的大纲。该课程旨在为学生提供深入了解商业和环境问题的交叉，以及与管理和营销相关的基本知识和技能。

在整个课程中，学生将参加互动讨论和活动，以加强他们的学习。到课程结束时，学生将获得全面的商业环境知识，以及与物流、管理、营销和商业管理相关的重要技能。
